❌ Transactions to IGNORE (GO+ noise)

Inside Touch 'n Go eWallet GO+, ignore ALL of these:

Quick Reload Payment (via GO+ Balance)
Fund Transfer
Any entry that is clearly internal GO+ routing / processing
Any duplicate legs of the same transaction (same amount + same time window + linked to a merchant payment)
Any “movement steps” that exist only to support a payment (not the final merchant or real counterparty)
